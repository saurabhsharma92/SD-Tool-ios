# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 2, 2026

## About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how System Design Refresher ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use this application. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

---

## Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google or Apple, Firebase Authentication stores your **email address** and **display name** to identify your account. Guest users have no account data on our servers.

The following data is stored **on-device only** and is never sent to our servers:
- Reading progress and bookmarks
- Favorite articles
- App settings and preferences
- AI usage counts (used to enforce daily quotas)
- Company blog visibility and pinning preferences

We do not collect device identifiers, location data, or usage analytics beyond what Firebase Authentication requires for authentication.

---

## AI Features

When you use Summary, Explain Simply, or Chat with Article, the text of the article you are reading is sent to Google's Gemini AI service to generate a response. No personal information (name, email, or account details) is included in these requests. AI features are entirely optional.

---

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

- **Google Firebase Authentication** — manages sign-in
- **Google Firebase AI Logic / Gemini** — powers AI features
- **GitHub Raw Content API** — serves articles, blog index, and flashcard content; no personal data is sent

We do not use advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or any other data-collection services.

---

## Data Storage & Retention

Account data (email, display name) is retained in Firebase Authentication for as long as your account exists. All other data — reading progress, favorites, settings — is stored locally on your device and deleted when you uninstall the app.

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time via **Settings → Delete Account**.

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you (your email / display name in Firebase)
- Correct inaccurate data by updating your Google or Apple account
- Delete your account and all associated data via Settings → Delete Account
- Use the app as a guest without providing any personal information

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you have additional rights under GDPR and CCPA respectively, including the right to data portability and the right to opt out of data sale. **We do not sell your data.**

---

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## Security

We rely on Firebase Authentication's industry-standard security for account data. Local data stored on your device is protected by iOS's built-in data protection mechanisms.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect when it was last revised. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
